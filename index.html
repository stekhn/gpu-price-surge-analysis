GPU Price Surge

Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies have climbed in value over the past year, thanks to increased demand from a range of investors. As the price of cryptocurrencies has increased, graphics card prices have skyrocketed.

GPUs Cryptocurrencies

Cryptocurrency miners are buying lots of mid- to high-end graphics cards to keep their operations profitable in very competetive market. The price for the average GPU was already above average in summer, but with the sudden increase in Bitcoin value and public interest before Christmas, we are in the middle of a second GPU price surge. The surge is fueled by an increasing demand for alternative cryptocurrencies, like Ethereum and Monero. Nvidia GTX 1070 was 410 Euro, now it's about 600 Euro
